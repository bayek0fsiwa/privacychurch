Our Privacy Policy
Q. What data do you collect and why?
A. We collect data about visits to our websites.

When you visit our website or service, regardless of whether you have an account or not, the website may use cookies, server logs, and other methods to collect the following data:

  • What pages you visit,
  • What actions you take on our website,
  • What browser, operating system, and device you use,
  • Search terms you use,

We use this data to:

  • Optimize websites and services, so that they are quick and easy to use,
  • Diagnose and debug technical errors,
  • Compile statistics on the popularity of a website, page, post, topic, etc., and
  • Compile statistics on the kinds of software and computers visitors use.

For all other inquiries, you can contact us via contact page.
